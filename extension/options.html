FT

Focused Tab Enforcer

Version 1.0.0

AI-powered exam monitoring extension that ensures academic integrity through real-time tab focus tracking and facial presence detection. All processing is performed locally — no video footage is transmitted.

Tab & window focus monitoring
Client-side face detection (face-api.js)
Head pose estimation
Anti-cheat keyboard/context-menu blocking
Offline violation queuing & sync
Real-time backend reporting

🔒 Privacy first: Face detection runs entirely in your browser. Only violation metadata (timestamps, types) — never images or video — is sent to the server.
